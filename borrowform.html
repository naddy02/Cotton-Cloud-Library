Book Borrowing Form

Rules and Guidlines

1. Library Membership

  • All users must be registered members of the library to borrow books.
  • A valid library card must be presented at the time of borrowing.

2. Borrowing Limit

  • Members can borrow up to 5 books at a time.
  • Different limits may apply for different membership types (e.g., student, faculty, general public).

3. Loan Period

  • The standard loan period is 2 weeks.
  • Certain high-demand or reference books may have shorter loan periods.

4. Renewals

  • Books can be renewed 3 times unless reserved by another member.
  • Renewal requests can be made in person, online, or by phone.

5. Reservations

  • Members can reserve books currently on loan.
  • Notifications will be sent when reserved books are available for pickup.
  • Reserved books must be picked up within 5 days.

6. Late Returns

  • A fine of RM5 per day will be charged for each overdue book.
  • Members with overdue books or unpaid fines may be restricted from borrowing additional books.

7. Lost or Damaged Books

  • Members are responsible for the care of borrowed books.
  • Lost or damaged books must be reported immediately.
  • Members will be charged the replacement cost of lost or damaged books.

8. Return Procedures

  • Books must be returned to the designated return area or book drop.
  • Ensure books are returned on or before the due date to avoid fines.
